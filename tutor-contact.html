Tutors are available to help students with relevant course material, assignments and any general engineering-related subject.

The help provided is mostly one-on-one. Tutors are all students who have taken the U0 and/or U1 course load of their department.

They are a great resource for not only course-related questions, but also for general advice.

Tutors may also hold review sessions before midterms and final exams, subject to demand in the office hours.
