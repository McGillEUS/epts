The Engineering Peer Tutoring Service is a free tutoring service for U0 and U1 engineering students, sponsored by the Faculty of Engineering.

Tutorial office hours are held on a weekly basis in the McGill Engineering Student Centre on the ground floor of the Frank Dawson Adams Building, Room 6B.

EPTS tutors can answer some of the most challenging questions and help give students what they need to obtain results in their respective field of study.

Update:

All EPTS Tutors will now have separate MyCourses pages that will allow students to directly interact with tutors, access helpful material that the tutor will post, and find all the Zoom links and announcements for the tutor's weekly office hours and exam review sessions.
